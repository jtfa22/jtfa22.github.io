BFR Capstone Project

BFR (acronym up to interpretation) is a <1 lb, single stage, active fin control rocket designed, built, and launched for the aerospace engineering capstone course, powered by a F50 motor, 3 12g digital servos, Teensy 4.1 microcontroller, AltIMU-10 sensor, and 2 1s LiPos.

Personally responsible for the controls design and implementation, electronics assembly and manufacturing, and flight software and testing.
